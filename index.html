Cyber Tracker

Protecting the Digital World

Our Mission

At Cyber Tracker, we aim to raise awareness to the serious digital threats that pose a risk to our privacy, security, and safety every day. In particular, we track data leaks over the years, as well as visualising the real, extensive impact of the internet. Most crucially, we offer cyber services to keep you protected.

link1, link2, and link3.



THE THREAT

Digital Safety across the world is vastly under-protected. It's predicted the global damages of cyber crime could increase to $10.5 trillion annually by 2025.

Imagine if someone could put your name into a search box and find out everything about you. Imagine if someone could push a button a change a red traffic light.

Imagine if someone could turn off life-support systems in a hospital.

The threat is rising.
